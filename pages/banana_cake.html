This is a banana cake recipe

This is what you will need

  • Plenty of bananas!
  • Flour, (also 2 cups)
  • Water, 10 ounces
  • Milk, 2 cups
  • Butter (plenty, as always)

here is how you make this delicious, fingerlicking dessert!

  1. Mix the powdered subtances
  2. Add the water
  3. Then add the rest...
  4. Sauce it all up w your mixture of bananas and milk!
  5. Add some walnuts on top to your liking (optional)
  6. Throw the batter in the oven for 40 minutes!
  7. After taking out the cake out of the oven, let it cool down for 10 minutes
  8. Enjoy!
